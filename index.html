SF Poker League

♠♥♦♣

A poker league for game developers and techies in San Francisco (inspired by the Silicon Valley Poker League). Invitation only - if you'd like to come, sign up at our Google group and tell us about yourself. Each member is allowed to bring one guest, if the member is present.

  • First tournament will be Wednesday, March 20th
  • Weekly tournaments on Mondays, starting April 1st
  • Food and drinks at 6:30pm
  • Cards in the air at 7pm
  • Finish game by 11pm sharp
  • Games are played at Kongregate HQ, at 2 Shaw Alley in downtown San Francisco.
  • $60 buy-in plus 2 rebuys in the first hour
  • $60 = starting stack of chips worth $1000
  • Blinds start at $5 and $10, going up every 15 minutes
  • The number of cashes and amount depends on number of players and rebuys.
  • Each player agrees in advance to donate 33% of their winnings to the prize pool (rounded to the nearest $20) and they get 1 point per $1 contributed.
  • Grand prize: Entry to a WSOP side event (we'll go for the main event next year)
  • If our champion cashes, he/she keeps 20% off the top and the other 80% is paid out accordingly to the points earned during the regular season.
  • Top 10 players in the regular season will play in a playoff tournament to determine the grand prize winner. The starting chips for each of the players will be their season points times 10.
